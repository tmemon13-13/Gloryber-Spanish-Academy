Who We Are · Quiénes Somos

More Than a Job — It's a Calling 💛

At GloryBer's Spanish Academy, we believe every child deserves a nurturing, bilingual environment where they can grow, explore, and thrive. Our team is the heart of everything we do — and we're always looking for caring, dedicated individuals to grow with us.

We are a licensed, home-based daycare and Spanish immersion academy serving infants through Pre-K in Alexandria, VA. If you are patient, joyful, bilingual (or learning!), and passionate about early childhood education, we'd love to hear from you.

Somos una guardería bilingüe con licencia, dedicada a brindar un ambiente cálido y enriquecedor para los más pequeños. Si eres una persona apasionada por la educación temprana, nos encantaría conocerte.
